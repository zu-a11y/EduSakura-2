My Activities

This page highlights the educational and cultural activities I experienced during my journey in Japan, from university visits to industrial tours, school exchange, and local immersion.

University Tour

During this educational trip, I had the opportunity to visit several universities in Japan and learn more about their academic environment, facilities, and student life.

Ritsumeikan University

Ritsumeikan University is a modern private university in Japan known for its international programs and innovative learning environment. It provides students with global opportunities and a supportive academic atmosphere.

Keio University

Keio University is one of Japan’s oldest and most respected private universities, known for its strong academic tradition and modern educational environment. It is recognized for producing many successful leaders and professionals in various fields.

Tokyo University

Tokyo University is a leading national university in Japan, renowned for its academic excellence and research contributions. It has a rich history and continues to be a hub for innovation and knowledge.

Other Activities

In addition to university visits, I also joined several memorable experiences that gave me a deeper understanding of Japanese culture, industry, and daily life.

Toyota Factory Tour

Visiting the Toyota factory gave me insight into Japan’s advanced manufacturing systems, teamwork, and efficiency. It was fascinating to observe how innovation and discipline come together in the production process.

Komazawa School

At Komazawa School, I was able to interact with local students and observe their school environment. This experience helped me compare educational cultures and appreciate the values of discipline and respect in Japanese schools.
